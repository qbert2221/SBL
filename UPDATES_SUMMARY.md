## Overview
All requested improvements have been implemented to increase credibility, trust, and conversion while maintaining the premium aesthetic.

## ✅ Changes Implemented

### 1. **Hero Section - More Credible Messaging**
**Location:** `index.html` - Hero section

**Before:**
> "A modern, asset-powered approach to home financing. Use a corporation, a stock-backed mortgage, and a leveraged dividend portfolio to acquire real estate without ever touching your paycheck."

**After:**
> "A private-capital strategy for investors who want to buy real estate without liquidating their portfolio or relying on monthly paycheck income."
> 
> "Model how pledged shares, dividend income, and corporate ownership can work together to cover the mortgage."

**Impact:** More precise, less promise-heavy, more credible.

---

### 2. **Trust Strip - Changed "Typical" to "Modeled"**
**Location:** `index.html` - Trust strip section

**Before:**
- "144% typical mortgage coverage"

**After:**
- "144% modeled coverage"
- Added clarifying note: "Based on sample inputs in the calculator. Your actual coverage will vary based on portfolio size, dividend yield, and leverage."

**Impact:** Removes unsubstantiated claim, adds transparency.

---

### 3. **NEW SECTION: "Who Is This For"**
**Location:** `index.html` - Added as section №03, before "How it works"

**Content:**
- Headline: "This is not a no-money-down mortgage hack."
- Clear explanation that this is for investors with existing portfolios
- Four qualifying bullets:
  - You own or control a meaningful stock portfolio
  - You want to buy real estate without liquidating assets
  - You care about cash flow, tax structure, and exit strategy
  - You are comfortable reviewing financing with lenders, tax advisors, and legal professionals

**Impact:** Filters the right buyer, increases credibility, sets proper expectations.

### 4. **Updated Language - "Monthly dividends pay the mortgage automatically"**
**Location:** `index.html` - The New Way comparison card

**Before:**
- "Monthly dividends pay the mortgage automatically"

**After:**
- "Monthly dividends are modeled against the mortgage payment"

**Impact:** Safer, more precise language that doesn't overpromise.

### 5. **NEW SECTION: Risk Reality**
**Location:** `index.html` - Added as section №05

**Content:**
- Headline: "The strategy is powerful, but not risk-free."
- Professional framing of six key risks:
  - Dividend income can change
  - Interest rates can rise
  - Portfolio value can drop
  - Leverage magnifies both upside and downside
  - Lender terms matter
  - Corporate structuring needs professional review

**Impact:** Increases trust with sophisticated investors, filters unqualified buyers, demonstrates professionalism.

### 7. **Calculator CTA Enhancement**
**Location:** `index.html` - Bottom CTA section

**Added:**
- "No signup required to test the model. Export the full report when you're ready."

**Impact:** Reduces friction, sets up future email capture strategy.

---

### 8. **Calculator Assumptions Box**
**Location:** `calculator.html` - Added immediately after page header

**Content:**
- Prominent "What this model assumes" callout box
- Lists all key assumptions:
  - Stable dividend payments at the rate you specify
  - No forced liquidation of pledged assets
  - Margin/loan rates remain within your selected input range
  - Taxes, fees, insurance, maintenance, and vacancy are not included
  - Portfolio value may fluctuate
  - Lender requirements and terms vary significantly
- Clear instruction: "Use these projections to model scenarios, then review implementation with qualified professionals."

**Impact:** Transforms calculator from "too magical" to "serious financial tool."

---

### 9. **Updated Disclaimers (Site-wide)**
**Location:** `index.html` and `calculator.html` - Footer legal text

**Before:**
> "Educational content only. Not financial advice. Securities lending involves risk including potential loss of pledged assets. Consult licensed professionals before implementing."

**After:**
> "Educational framework only. SBL Mortgage does not provide tax, legal, investment, or lending advice. Use the calculator to model scenarios, then review any implementation with qualified professionals. Securities lending involves risk including potential loss of pledged assets."

**Impact:** More specific, clearer boundaries, professional tone.
